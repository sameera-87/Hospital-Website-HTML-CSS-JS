Our History

Since its inception in 1995, Kings Hospital has been at the helm of Colombo's healthcare. What was initially a vision of offering quality healthcare to individuals and families grew into one of the most trusted private healthcare facilities in the country. In due course, we expanded our services, invested in state-of-the-art technology, and hired expert specialists to meet the growing healthcare needs of the community.

Our Vision

To be the leading private healthcare provider in Sri Lanka, recognized for excellence in patient care, medical innovation, and community wellness. We aim to set the standard for quality healthcare services and be a trusted partner in the journey to better health.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional healthcare services with compassion, integrity, and professionalism, ensuring that every patient receives the best possible care. We are committed to improving the health and well-being of the people we serve by offering advanced medical treatment, expert consultations, and personalized care.
